# Content Writing Tips (Tanveer Nadla)

## Content Writing:
- Intro / First paragraph
  - Article ki summary first para mein provide krdein.
  - 1st para highly optimized, entity-rich, not too lengthy (3-4 lines).
  - AEO also depends on the 1st para (it should contain the solution).
  - Intro should be solution-oriented.

- Google NLP Analysis:
    - For finding entities (entities topic ey related term)
    - How does Google understand? Google understands content by "entities surrounding your keyword."
    - Sentiment Analysis:
    - Find Content categories

- Topical Authority (Pints that will distinguish you from competitors):/ Value Addition
  Jab bhi ap kisi topic pey likh rhy hon tw us topic sey relavant cheezon ka apko pta hona chahiye
   - For this, you have to research that niche
   - Example: aAp kisi app k barey mein likh rhy hon tw apko us
   - - app ka pta hona chahiye even error kiya atey hein,
     - what people like most about the app
     - Is the app paid or free? Kis area mein paid hae aur kis mein free?
     - Is the app banned in any area?
    
- Use ChatGPT to find entities:
  - prompt:
    `I am optimising article on "uour keyword" can you enlist entites which i can use in my content/article? to outrank my competitor`
   - second Method:
      - apney competitors k 5-10 articles dedo chatgpt ko aur phr usko kaho k ispey nlp analysis kr k entities nikal k dedo
